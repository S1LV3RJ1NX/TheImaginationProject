What is This?

You enter a chamber. You don't know what's inside. You look around, examine objects, talk to strangers, read inscriptions. Clues aren't handed to you — they're discovered.

The game asks one question: What do you do?

You type your actions in plain English. The world responds. Some chambers test your deduction. Others test moral judgment, persuasion, or patience. Often, the answer was right in front of you — you just had to look closer.

Think D&D exploration meets escape rooms — no dice, no character sheets. Just your curiosity and your imagination.
